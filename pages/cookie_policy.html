Cookie Policy

Hello,
According to EU law (and common courtesy) I'm obliged to tell you that I use Cookies for cetain aspects of this site. As I'm gradually building this thing I will update this page whenever new functionality has any effect on your privacy.

At present, my site stores two cookies on your computer:

  • Cookie Consent Notice
    Somewhat ironically, the pop-up banner that lets you know I'm using cookies uses a cookie to remember it's already popped up...
  • Google Analytics
    In order to tell the difference between new and returning visitors, I use a cookie to anonymously distinguish between users of the site.
    This also gives a general idea on what pages/posts are most popular and how things change over time.
    You can opt-out of this here.

I do also read some cookies that are already on your computer/mobile/whatever. This is also part of Google Analytics, but is based on data from other sites, as opposed to what you do on mine. Again, its anonymous and I have no idea who you all are, but I can get a rough idea whether my site is of more interest to certain age groups or genders.

As you can see, there are no adverts on my site and while I can host it for free on good ol' GitHub pages, that will remain the case. I'm therefore not sharing any of the analytics data with advertisers, but (because it's just a useful thing to know) you can find out more & opt out of those sorts of things here.
