About the blog

What's in a name?

Personal

My son has had many nicknames as he's grown from a Speck in the womb, through an entire salad of fruit & veg that the books use for size comparisons, and finally to Sharky. An odd name, you may think, but his flipping and wiggling in the last months before birth were more than a little reminiscent of a shark in it's egg-case.

The blog then, is named in his honour and as my wife and I don't really want photos of our boy all over the internet (they'd spread like wildfire - he's like a chubby Chris Evans crossed with a big-eyed Chris Hemsworth) I've decided to accompany the posts with doodles instead. Now, humans have never been part of my very limited artistic repertoir, but sea creatures I can do... sort of.


So, here we go, take a look at the blog page and you'll find what I hope will be a humerous account of me trying to cope with my son growing up.

Please feel free to share the posts using the social media buttons and, if I manage to implement it, you can pose questions using a link I'll add to the navigation menu.
